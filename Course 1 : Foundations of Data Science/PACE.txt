Technical and Strategic data professional roles :
Technical data professionals :
  1. Involves Mathematics, Statistics and Computing. Explores datasets and builds Models.
  2. Data Scientists, Machine Learning Engineers, Statisticians.
Strategic data professionals :
  1. Interpret information for organization's operations, finance, research and development.
  2. BI professionals, Technical Project Managers

PACE - Plan, Analyze, Construct, Execute :
  1. Communicate with stakeholders and data team to create a project proposal and plan deadlines, budget, resource availability and discuss the known issues. Stick to the purpose.
  2. Analyze the data for any missing data and perform EDA.
  3. Construct - build the model according to the requirement and provide the results.
  4. Execute - prepare dashboards and present it to the audience by storytelling.
